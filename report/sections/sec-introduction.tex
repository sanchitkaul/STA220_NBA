\section{Introduction}

In professional basketball, a team's real character is often revealed when the game is 
on the line(final minutes). While regular season records provide a broad measure of a team’s overall quality, they sometimes 
don't capture how a squad responds when every possession carries immense weight. This project, titled 
``When the Game Is on the Line: A Statistical Analysis of NBA Clutch Performance,'' investigates whether success in
high-pressure moments is a simple byproduct of being a ``good team'' or if it is a measurable trait driven by 
some statistical factors.
